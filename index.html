Welcome

Poetry and Place: Chicago (PPC) is a digital repository designed to highlight Chicago through poets, their respective poetry, and the locative elements that are the subjects of their works. Its purpose through digitized poetry and images is to educate and enlighten students, educators, and general audiences on the literary rich and familiar city, Chicago.

New

One of Chicago most famous cemeteries, Graceland Cemetary has been added to our site as a location made even more significant in Marge Piercy's "A Visit to a Dead Man on a Summer Day." Be sure you see the great images of Graceland after reading Piercy's work.
